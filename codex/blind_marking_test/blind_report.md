## Main Lesson

This blind rerun is cleaner than the first attempt, but it shows a different problem: when given the full solutions document, I can become much more generous than the human marker, especially for polished answers that sit close to the scheme’s wording and structure.

The strongest over-marking happened on Fahima’s:

- `Part 2 Q2`
- `Part 2 Q3`
- `Part 3 Q3`

So the next useful calibration step is not “can I read the scheme”, but “how do I stop awarding near-solution marks when the student answer is formally good but still short of the human standard”.
